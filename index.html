Revolutionary Features

🤖

Apple Vision AI

Advanced AI background removal using Apple's Vision Framework. Your clothes are automatically extracted from photos with perfect precision, all processed locally on your iPhone.

🔒

100% Local Storage

Complete privacy by design. Your wardrobe data never leaves your device. No cloud dependency, no tracking, no data sharing. Your style, your privacy.

🎨

Smart Canvas

Intelligent outfit creation with drag & drop interface. Pinch-to-zoom, automatic positioning, and smart flat-lay arrangement. Create stunning outfits in under 30 seconds.

Born from a Real Problem

I love fashion, but I hate spending 20 minutes every morning deciding what to wear. Existing wardrobe apps were complex, slow, and didn't understand how we actually get dressed.

That's how GARMI was born. In just 62 days, I built a digital wardrobe that's simple, intelligent, and respects your privacy. Using Apple's cutting-edge AI, GARMI makes outfit creation effortless and fun.

This project was developed as part of my Portfolio Project at Holberton School, where I learned to think like a developer and build solutions that matter.
